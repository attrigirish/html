Cabinet Reshuffle: PM Modi Meets With 9 New Ministers, Spotlight On Defence - 10 Points

All India | Reported by Akhilesh Sharma

Four Ministers of State with Independent Charge will be promoted to the Union Cabinet, sources said. They are, Commerce Minister Nirmala Sitharaman, Power Minister Piyush Goyal, Petroleum Minister Dharmendra Pradhan and Minority Affairs Minister Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi.
